## iFlash Flashcards

iFlash is our spaced repetition system (SRS) that optimizes your learning.

### How It Works
1. **Review Cards**: Tap "iFlash" to start a review session
2. **Read the Question**: Read the flashcard content
3. **Rate Your Response**: Choose how well you knew the answer:
   - **Again** (Red): You didn't know it - review soon
   - **Hard** (Orange): You knew it but struggled - review in a few days
   - **Good** (Blue): You knew it well - review in a week
   - **Easy** (Green): You knew it perfectly - review in a month

### Card Types
- **Multiple Choice**: Select the correct answer from options
- **Term/Definition**: Flip the card to reveal the answer
- **Fill in the Blank**: Type your answer

### Tips for Success
- Review cards regularly (daily is best)
- Don't rush - take time to think
- Use "Hard" liberally - better safe than sorry
- Focus on cards marked "Due" first

## Quizzes

Practice quizzes help you prepare for the real exam.

### Taking a Quiz
1. Tap the "Quiz" tab
2. Select a question bank
3. Choose your settings:
   - **Timed** (recommended): Set a time limit
   - **Untimed**: Take as long as you need
4. Tap "Start Quiz"

### During the Quiz
- Answer each question
- Use "Flag" to mark questions to review later
- See your progress at the top
- Tap "Next" to continue

### After the Quiz
- See your score and percentage
- Review answers by topic
- Identify weak areas
- Retake to improve

## Offline Mode

Study anywhere, even without internet!

### How It Works
1. All content is cached on your device
2. Your reviews and quiz answers are queued
3. When you reconnect, everything syncs automatically

### Using Offline
1. Download content while online
2. Turn off WiFi/mobile data
3. Study as normal
4. Your progress is saved locally
5. Reconnect to sync

### What Syncs
- Flashcard reviews
- Quiz answers
- Lesson progress
- Chat messages
- Achievements

## FAQ

### Q: How often should I study?
**A**: Daily is ideal! Even 15-30 minutes per day is better than cramming. The spaced repetition system works best with consistent daily practice.

### Q: Can I reset my progress?
**A**: Contact support to reset your progress. This will clear all your reviews and quiz scores.

### Q: How does the spaced repetition system work?
**A**: Cards are reviewed at increasing intervals based on how well you know them. Cards you struggle with are reviewed more frequently.

### Q: Can I use the app offline?
**A**: Yes! All content is cached locally. Your reviews and quiz answers will sync when you reconnect.

### Q: What's the difference between "Hard" and "Again"?
**A**: "Again" means you didn't know the answer - it will be reviewed very soon. "Hard" means you knew it but struggled - it will be reviewed in a few days.

### Q: How do I unlock premium features?

1. **Study Daily**: Consistency beats intensity. 15 minutes daily > 2 hours once a week
